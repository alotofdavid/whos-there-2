About Who's There?

Our app aims to notify people via text, email or a simple notification without even taking your phone out of your pocket. With the ease of Who's There? you can send notifications to your friends by simply knocking on your phone.

Our Vision

This app was developed during a cross-college hackathon at UC Berkley - Cal Hacks. The four developers Myles Scolnick, David Adams, Weston Mizumoto, Dennis Fang worked for many hours straight building a simple solution to sending notifications without taking your phone out of your pocket.


Fast

With Who's There? you can send notifications instantly without opening your phone.

Friendly

Who's There? allows you to connect to all your friends via Facebook to send knocks to everyone.

Customizable

You can select any number of people to be sent a notification for any number of seqential knocks.
